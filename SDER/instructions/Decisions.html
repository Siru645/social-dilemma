Starting Balances and Random Number Draws

In this experiment, each participant has an Individual Account, and each group of 4 shares a Group Account. The experiment begins with 10 tokens in each Individual Account. The initial number of tokens in the Group Account is unknown to you, between 0 and 40. Before your decision, you will also be assigned a random number between 0 and 10. This number of tokens will be added to the starting balance of the Group Account. The remaining tokens out of 10 will be paid to you as part of your final payoff. This process happens for each member, but you will only know your own number.

  • Tokens are worth 3 ECUs in the Group Account (0.75 ECUs per group member)
  • Tokens are worth 1 ECU in your Individual Account

Binding Decisions

Because you only know the random number of tokens drawn for you, and not the other members of your group, you will not know the initial balance of the Group Account before you make your decision. So, we ask you to submit binding decisions covering all possible starting balances of the Group Account.

Decision Task

Each person will privately decide to either:

  • move up to 10 tokens from their Individual Account to the Group Account, or
  • move up to 1/4 of the tokens from the Group Account to their Individual Account

Earnings

After all individuals have made their decision, you will be told the average random number drawn among the other 3 group members, which of your decisions is played, the final Group Account balance, and your earnings from the Group and Individual Accounts. The final value of the Group Account will be divided equally among all individuals in the group. For each individual in the group, their total earnings will be combined with the amount that the individual has remaining in their Individual Account from Decision 1 and equally distributed final Group Account.

Your total earnings will be the remaining tokens drawn after the random number of tokens is added to the Group Account, the final value of tokens (in ECUs) in your Individual Account, and one-fourth of the final value of tokens in the Group Account (in ECUs).

Starting Balances

In this experiment, each participant has an Individual Account, and each group of 4 shares a Group Account. The experiment begins with 10 tokens in each Individual Account and 0 tokens in the Group Account.

  • Tokens are worth 3 ECUs in the Group Account (0.75 ECUs per group member)
  • Tokens are worth 1 ECU in your Individual Account

Decision Task

Each person will privately decide to move between 0 and 10 tokens from their Individual Account to the Group Account. Each decision must be in whole tokens.

Earnings

After all individuals finish their Decision Task, you will be told the average and the total tokens contributed to the Group Account and the final Group Account balance, and your earnings from the Group and Individual Accounts. The final value of the Group Account will be divided equally among all individuals in the group. For each individual in the group, earnings in the Group Account will be combined with the earnings remained in their Individual Account.

Your total earnings will be the final value of tokens (in ECUs) in your Individual Account plus one-fourth of the final value of tokens in the Group Account (in ECUs).

Decision 1
Starting Balances

In this experiment, each participant has an Individual Account, and each group of 4 shares a Group Account. The experiment begins with 10 tokens in each Individual Account and 0 tokens in the Group Account.

  • Tokens are worth 3 ECUs in the Group Account (0.75 ECUs per group member)
  • Tokens are worth 1 ECU in your Individual Account

Decision Task

Each person will privately decide to move between 0 and 10 tokens from their Individual Account to the Group Account. Each decision must be in whole tokens.

Earnings

Any tokens left in your Individual Account after Decision 1 will be converted to U.S. dollars using a conversion rate of $1 for every {{exchange_rate}} ECUs at the end of the experiment. Tokens left in the Individual Account after Decision 1 cannot be used for Decision 2.

Decision 2
Starting Balances

In Decision 2, each individual begins with 10 tokens in their Individual Account. Each four-person group begins Decision 2 with the Group Account value determined by the sum of each member's Decision 1.

  • Tokens are worth 3 ECUs in the Group Account (0.75 ECUs per group member)
  • Tokens are worth 1 ECU in your Individual Account

Binding Decisions

You will not know the total amount of tokens in the Group Account from Decision 1 until you finish Decision 2. Therefore, you will have to submit a binding decision for each possible average number of tokens that the other members in your group moved to the Group Account.

Decision Task

Each person will privately decide to either:

  • move up to 10 tokens from their Individual Account to the Group Account, or
  • move up to 1/4 of the tokens from the Group Account to their Individual Account

Earnings

The final value of the Group Account will be divided equally among all individuals in the group. For each individual in the group, those earnings will be combined with the amount that the individual had remaining in their own Individual Account from both Decision 1 and Decision 2. Your total earnings will be the final value of tokens (in ECUs) in your own Individual Account from both Decision 1 and Decision 2 plus one-fourth of the final value of tokens in the Group Account (in ECUs).

Results

After all individuals have made Decision 2, you will be told the average Decision 1 of the other members in your group, which of your decisions is played in Decision 2, the final Group Account balance, and your earnings from each account.
