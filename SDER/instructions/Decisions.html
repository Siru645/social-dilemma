{{if treatment == 'M'}} Starting Balances

In this experiment, each participant has an Individual Account, and each group of 4 shares a Group Account. The experiment begins with each Individual Account containing 10 tokens. Each token is worth 1 ECU in your Individual Account, for a total value of 10 ECUs. The initial number of tokens in the Group Account is unknown to you, between 0 and 40.

Binding Decisions

You will not know the exact balance of the group account until you finish the Decision Task. Therefore, you will have to submit a binding decision for each possible number of tokens in the starting Group Account.

Decision Task

Each person will privately choose to move tokens between their Individual Account and the Group Account. Each person can move up to a maximum of 10 tokens to the Group Account from their Individual Account, or a maximum of 1/4 of the tokens in the Group Account to their Individual Account.

  • Tokens added to or removed from the Group Account change the Group Account value by 3 ECUs.
  • Tokens added to or removed from the Individual Account change the Individual Account value by 1 ECU.
Earnings

After all individuals have made their decision, you will be informed of the average group balance and which of your decisions is played, the final Group Account balance, and your earnings from the Group and Individual Accounts. The final value of the Group Account will be divided equally among all individuals in the group. For each individual in the group, their total earnings will be combined with the amount that the individual has remaining in their own Individual Account from Decision 1 and equally distributed final Group Account.

Your total earnings will be the final value of tokens (in ECUs) in your own Individual Account and one-fourth of the final value of tokens in the Group Account (in ECUs).

Starting Balances

In this experiment, each participant has an Individual Account, and each group of 4 shares a Group Account. The experiment begins with each Individual Account containing 10 tokens. Each token is worth 1 ECU in your Individual Account, for a total value of 10 ECUs. The Group Account begins with a starting balance of 0 tokens, and therefore has a value of 0 ECUs.

Decision Task

Each person will decide independently and privately whether to move any of their tokens from their own Individual Account into the Group Account. Each person can move up to a maximum of 10 tokens to the Group Account. Each token that a person adds to the Group Account reduces the value of their Individual Account by 1 ECU. However, each token added to the Group Account by a group member increases the value of the Group Account by 3 ECUs. Each decision must be in whole tokens.

Any tokens left in your Individual Account after Decision 1 will be converted to U.S. dollars using a conversion rate of $1 for every [exchange rate] ECUs at the end of the experiment. Tokens left in the Individual Account after Decision 1 cannot be used for Decision 2.

Once all individuals in the group have completed Decision 1, the group will move to Decision 2.

Decision 2
Starting Balances

In Decision 2, each individual begins with a starting balance of 10 tokens worth 1 ECU each, for a total value of 10 ECUs. Each four-person group begins with the Group Account value determined by Decision 1.

Binding Decisions

However, you will not know the total amount of tokens in the Group Account in Decision 1 until you finish Decision 2. Therefore, you will have to submit a binding decision for each possible average number of tokens that other members moved to the Group Account.

Decision Task

Each person will privately choose to move tokens between their Individual Account and the Group Account. Each person can move up to a maximum of 10 tokens to the Group Account from their Individual Account, or a maximum of 1/4 of the tokens in the Group Account to their Individual Account.

  • Tokens added to or removed from the Group Account change the Group Account value by 3 ECUs.
  • Tokens added to or removed from the Individual Account change the Individual Account value by 1 ECU.
Earnings

After all individuals have made Decision 2, you will be informed of the average Decision 1 and which of your decisions is played in Decision 2, the final Group Account balance, and your earnings from the Group and Individual Accounts. The final value of the Group Account will be divided equally among all individuals in the group. For each individual in the group, those earnings will be combined with the amount that the individual has remaining in their own Individual Account from both Decision 1 and Decision 2.

Your total earnings will be the final value of tokens (in ECUs) in your own Individual Account from both Decision 1 and Decision 2 plus one-fourth of the final value of tokens in the Group Account (in ECUs).
